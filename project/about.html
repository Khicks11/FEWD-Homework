About Eim Modern

People of any age want to own things of value. As a collector you trust your eye and acquire unique items that are a reflection of your personality and passions. As an investor of the unique you strive to accumulate items that will increase in value over time. The same sought after Mid-Century chair of today will be harder to find as years go by, thus will retain or increase its value. The same can not be said for many mass produced items available at big box stores of inferior quality.

When we find an object that grabs our attention due to its design, material, and make, we try to research the story behind the design. To realize some handmade items take hundreds of hours of labor to create the finished product makes one appreciate the visual aesthetics even more and one can conclude why it is collectible today.

We try to accurately describe each item and calculate a fair reasonable price based on condition, investment in item, rarity, and known desirability. We know others will appreciate the items as much as we do.
